# About

Playing the piano has been a childhood dream of mine — something I always wanted to do but never had the chance to pursue. In November 2024, I finally decided to make it happen.

I am entirely self-taught. Most of my learning comes from watching YouTube videos, studying how others play, and learning by ear. I'm still working on reading sheet music, so a lot of what I play is learned by observation and repetition rather than from notation.

This section of the site is where I share pieces I've been working on. They are not perfect, and that's okay — this is a journal of progress, not a recital.
